Continuity Check

Keep the same world in every scene

Use product and character reference sheets to lock the details that must not change.

  • Product shape, label, material and color
  • Character face, age, hair and clothing
  • Scene order, timing and voiceover
Negative Prompt

Tell the model what must not happen

The new multi-select guardrails automatically add common quality and continuity restrictions to your prompt.

  • No distorted hands, flicker or identity drift
  • No changed packaging or invented claims
  • No cropped product or unreadable generated text
